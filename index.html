Welcome

This site is hosted to allow for ease in listenting police scanner audio streams. Use of police scanners makes for a neat hobby, however, the startup cost can be great. That is where this site comes in; so sit back, relax, and enjoy!

Currently this site provides access to 2 audio streams as well as links to find more and become involved in this hobby. Check below the streams to see more information. Please SHARE this site!

CNU Audio Stream:

The first audio stream is that of Christopher Newport University (CNU) Police Department; CNU is a small liberal arts college in Newport news, Virgina. The scanner station is located approximately 1 mile from the tower. CNUPD is on the City of Newport News digitally trunked (P25) system.

Groups on this stream: CNUPD Dispatch & CNUPD Special Events

Newport News Audio Stream:

The second stream is that of the City of Newport News; the city is located in southeastern Virginia as a part of the Hampton Roads area. The scanner station is located approximately 8-10 miles from the tower. The city is on the Newport News digitally trunked (P25) system.

Groups on this stream: Fire Dispatch & Fire Tactical (2-6) & Mutual Aid (1-4) & Marine Incident Response Team

Hobby Information:

This portion will be updated as I develop my skills in this hobby.

First and formost, one of the great resources is the following site: Radio Reference. The two main important peices of the site is the Database section, and the Wiki.

For now, take a look around there and become familiar with some terminology. My personal preference for purchases of the scanners is Amazon where I was able to purchase a WS1040 (made by Whistler) and that is what is currently behind the CNU Stream. The Newport News stream is using a Radio Shack Pro-106.

Note that the Hampton Roads area is on an APCO P-25 system, meaning that it is Digitally Trunked and so the scanner must support that technology. However, a cheap scanner may be used to pickup the local fire department simulcasts, aircrafts, ATC, and even a fast food drive-thru!
